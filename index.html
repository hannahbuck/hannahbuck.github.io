ABOUT GALLERY CALENDAR

Life drawing sessions - Thursdays 7:30-9:30pm

Weekly untutored drop-in life drawing sessions starting from April 6th at Manor Farm Community Hut, HA4 8BD. Artists of all levels welcome!

What to bring

Drawing boards, seating and a model will all be provided. Basic art materials (pencils, paper and charcoal) will be available for purchase, but please feel free to bring your own.

Getting to the hut

We're about 10 minutes' walk from Ruislip Underground Station.
